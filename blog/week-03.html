Building a lighting edge sign

For my personal homework project, I decided to build a glowing edge light sign. It features a box, housing a LED strip and power supply, together with an engraved sheet of acrylic. By lighting the acrylic sheet from below, the engraved motive on the acrylic plate appears to be glowing. Using the Trotec 400 laser cutter, I manufactured the press-fit parametric box from 2,5mm MDF plates. As the time slots were heavily compressed when using the laser cutter, the acrylic sign was not manufactured on that day.
